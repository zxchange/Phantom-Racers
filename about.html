About Phantom Racers

Phantom Racers is a passionate iRacing team dedicated to pushing the limits of virtual racing. Founded in 2024, we have quickly risen to become a formidable force in the iRacing community.

Our team is made up of talented drivers from around the world, each bringing their own unique skills and experiences to the track.
